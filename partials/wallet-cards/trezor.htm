Step 1: Navigate to TREZOR wallet

Open your TREZOR wallet by navigating to https://wallet.trezor.io/. Make sure you have your USB device plugged in.

Step 2: Create a new empty account

From the currency selection list, make sure you have Bitcoin Cash (BCH) selected. Below the list of accounts, click the Add Account link to create a brand new account. The new account will be assigned a number, for example, Account #5.

Step 3: Locate account public key (XPUB)

Above the list of accounts, click your device name to be taken to the Device basics screen. Click the Show XPUBs button to reveal the public keys. Click on the Account number you created in Step 2 and copy the value to your clipboard.

Step 4: Input the XPUB key

Paste the XPUB key from your clipboard in to the form directly below these instructions. Click the Check key button to observe the address sequence.

Step 5: Verify the address sequence

From the TREZOR wallet, click the Account number and the Receive tab. Click the More addresses button three (3) times. Make sure the addresses shown on the TREZOR wallet match exactly with the address sequence shown below.

All done! Click Save key and start enjoying the features of TXButton.
