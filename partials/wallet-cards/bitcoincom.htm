Step 1: Open the Bitcoin.com Wallet app

Download and install the Bitcoin.com Wallet app from your device's marketplace. Proceed through the initial wallet set up instructions.

Step 2: Create a new empty wallet

From the Home screen, tap the plus symbol (+) next to the Wallets heading. Select New personal wallet and assign the wallet with a name. Toggle the Bitcoin Cash radio control. Click Create new wallet.

Step 3: Locate account public key (XPUB)

From the Settings screen, tap on your newly created wallet under the Wallets & Integrations section. Scroll to the bottom and tap More Options then Wallet Information. Scroll to the bottom and tap on the "xpub" code below the Extended Public Keys section. The code will be saved to your clipboard.

Step 4: Input the XPUB key

Open TXButton.com using your device browser, sign in to your account and tap Wallet Settings. Paste the XPUB key from your clipboard in to the form. Tap the Check key button to observe the first address in the sequence.

Step 5: Verify the address sequence

From the Bitcoin.com wallet, tap the Back arrow and tap Wallet Addresses Tap the plus symbol (+) next to the Unused Addresses heading three (3) times. Make sure the addresses shown on this screen match exactly with with the address sequence shown on the TXButton Wallet Settings page.

All done! Click Save key and start enjoying the features of TXButton.
